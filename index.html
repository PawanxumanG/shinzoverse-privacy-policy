Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

ShinzoVerse ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the ShinzoVerse mobile application and related services.

Information We Collect

When using ShinzoVerse, we may collect the following information:

  • Gaming UID or user identification information
  • Device information (device type, OS version)
  • Gameplay activity and tournament participation
  • Account information required to operate the platform
  • Wallet transaction records including rewards and withdrawals

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the ShinzoVerse platform
  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Organize and manage skill-based gaming tournaments
  • Track rewards, wallet balance, and tournament winnings
  • Process withdrawal requests through supported payment methods such as UPI or bank transfer
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized activity

Wallet, Rewards, and Withdrawals

ShinzoVerse includes a digital wallet system used to track tournament rewards and winnings. Users may request withdrawals of their winnings through supported payment methods such as UPI or bank transfer where available.

All wallet transactions are recorded for security, verification, and operational purposes.

Third-Party Services

ShinzoVerse may use trusted third-party services to operate certain features of the platform. These services may include:

  • Firebase (authentication, database, analytics)
  • Payment processing services for withdrawals

These third-party services have their own privacy policies governing the use of data.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect user information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Children’s Privacy

ShinzoVerse is intended for users aged 18 years and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from users under the age of 18.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
